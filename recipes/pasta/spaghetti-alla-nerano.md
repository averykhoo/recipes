# Spaghetti alla Nerano

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/285205/that-zucchini-spaghetti-stanley-tucci-loves-spaghetti-alla-nerano/
https://www.newyorker.com/culture/kitchen-notes/the-secrets-of-stanley-tuccis-zucchini-spaghetti

Yields 2 portions

## Ingredients

* 2 green zucchini, sliced into 0.8 cm (~1/3 inch) slices
* 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
* 1 pinch salt
* 120g (~4 oz) spaghetti
* 1/2 cup milk
* ~1 tsp black pepper
* 1/4 cup grated cheddar
* 6 basil leaves, torn into small pieces
* 4 tablespoons grated Pecorino Romano / Parmegiano / Grana Padano cheese
* black pepper to taste

## Directions

1. Add oil and salt to zucchini and bake at 220c for 45 minutes, turning once or twice to let steam out
2. Boil the pasta with some salt
3. Reserve some pasta water
4. Add zucchini, milk, pepper, and cheese, and stir to combine
5. Tear in the basil near the end
6. Add pasta water as needed to loosen the sauce and adjust the saltiness
7. Serve on a plate, topped with grated cheese and then with pepper
