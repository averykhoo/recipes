# Mushroom Soup

https://www.allrecipes.com/recipe/235589/chef-johns-creamy-mushroom-soup/

## Ingredients 

* 600g mushrooms
* 1 onion
* 3 Tbsp butter
* 1 Tbsp flour
* 2 cloves garlic
* herbs
* 2-3 cups stock 
* 1 cup cream
* salt and pepper

## Directions 

1. fry mushrooms and onion
2. add garlic and flour
3. boil with stock but no cream 
4. finish with cream
