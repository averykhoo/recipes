# 100 second poached yellow croaker a la putien

1. fillet the fish
2. pressure cook the fish bones and bits for maybe an hour (about 1.5-2l water), then take out just the clear soup 
3. boil 2 slices ginger, half an onion, 1tsp miso, and 1tsp sake with the fish 
4. scoop out the fish into a bowl until ready to serve
5. pour hot soup onto fish and garnish with parsley to serve
