
* 200-300g beansprouts
* 1 tbsp tahini
* 2 tbsp toasted sesame oil
* 3 tbsp shoyu
* 1 tsp vinegar
* 1/4 tsp pepper
* 1/8 (or less) tbsp shichimi togarashi
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 1/4 tsp sugar

1. blanch and drain beansprouts
2. mix sauce
3. toss with beansprouts to coat
4. fry together until the sauce is stuck to the beansprouts
