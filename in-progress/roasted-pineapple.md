# roasted pineapple

## ingredients

* 1 pineapple, peeled and de-eyed
* 1/4 cup sugar / syrup
* 2 tsp cinnamon
* water, juice, or a splash of wine or rum, optional, to loosen up the marinade

## directions

1. mix the marinade
2. baste and roast at 190C for 15 mins
3. repeat
4. repeat
